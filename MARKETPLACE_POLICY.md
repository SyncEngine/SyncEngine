# SyncEngine Marketplace Policy

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to all modules submitted for distribution through the [SyncEngine Marketplace](https://marketplace.syncengine.io/).  
Modules are installable extensions that run within SyncEngine and extend its functionality.

## 2. Submission Guidelines

- Modules must not modify the core SyncEngine software.
- Modules must be installable via SyncEngine’s standard installation mechanism.
- Modules must comply with SyncEngine’s technical, security, and quality standards.
- SyncEngine may provide additional guidelines for compatibility and security certification.

## 3. Intellectual Property

- Module authors retain all rights to their modules.
- SyncEngine does not require modules to be open-source; closed-source modules are permitted.
- Authors are responsible for compliance with applicable laws, licenses, and third-party dependencies.

## 4. Marketplace Participation

- Use of the Marketplace is optional. Authors may distribute modules independently.
- Modules distributed through the Marketplace may be subject to revenue sharing and additional terms, which will be communicated during submission.

## 5. Removal of Modules

SyncEngine reserves the right to remove or disable modules that:

- Violate applicable laws or marketplace policies.
- Contain malicious or harmful code.
- Misrepresent their functionality or breach technical standards.
